About Tempest:

If you are looking to work in an exciting environment on high growth brands spread across challenging sectors like hospitality, education, micro irrigation, large township projects, metro rail, real estate, agri-tech, building materials, and even social and developmental sectors, you will find it at Tempest Advertising.

We are a highly awarded national agency with branches in Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Pune. Our team of professionals, drawn from Indian and Multi-national companies, are focused on delivering value to our clients across traditional and digital platforms.

Stable yet aggressive teams have ensured better business, leading to better growth for the company and employees. And, incidentally, we are the guys who won the national contest to re-design the logo of Advertising Agencies Association of India, beating over 250 plus agencies from across India.

Our Way:

Tempest Advertising is not only driven by impactful strategy and creative solutions but is also deeply rooted in social, family, and cultural values & ethics. Being a gender-balanced workplace, the work culture we have developed ensures a friendly and supportive environment, extending opportunities equally for women, mothers, as well as working parents, to achieve the right balance between work and family.

For more details visit us at: www.tempestadvertising.com

As a Jr. Art Director at Tempest, you will have the opportunity to work alongside seasoned creatives and contribute your unique perspective to the development of engaging concepts and captivating designs. We value accuracy, efficiency, organization, collaboration, and most importantly, your creative instincts. You will be responsible for translating ideas into visually stunning designs that resonate with our target audience across various platforms and mediums.

Responsibilities:

  • Utilize your visual thinking skills to generate fresh and innovative concepts that captivate and engage our audience.

  • Produce excellent designs that effectively communicate the desired message across different platforms and mediums.

  • Actively participate and contribute creative ideas during brainstorming sessions, collaborating with cross-functional teams to bring ideas to life.

  • Transform concepts into compelling designs that leave a lasting impact on our clients and their customers.

  • Demonstrate a hunger for learning, continuously improving your craft, and staying up-to-date with industry trends and techniques.

  • Collaborate with senior creatives to devise effective creative strategies that align with our clients' goals and objectives.

  • Work closely with photographers, printers, and production houses to ensure the seamless execution of design projects.

Requirements:

  • Passion for advertising and a genuine enthusiasm for creating impactful and meaningful campaigns.

  • 3-4 years of experience in the advertising industry, demonstrating a solid understanding of design principles and trends.

  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, etc.) to create visually stunning designs.

  • Excellent design skills, with a meticulous attention to detail and the ability to translate complex ideas into visually compelling assets.

  • Ability to work under pressure and deliver high-quality work within tight deadlines.

  • Strong research and listening skills, enabling you to understand clients' needs and preferences.

  • Effective communication and presentation skills, allowing you to articulate and advocate for your creative vision.

  • Ability to collaborate effectively with the client servicing team, ensuring client satisfaction and successful project delivery.

  • Originality, accountability, calmness, and confidence to handle multiple projects and responsibilities simultaneously.

  • Advertising agency know-how, understanding the dynamics and processes involved in a fast-paced creative environment.

  • A strong creative portfolio that showcases your skills and demonstrates your ability to conceptualize and execute engaging designs.

Additional Requirement:

  • Proficiency in video editing software and experience in creating compelling video content would be an added advantage.

Note: Please provide a link to your creative portfolio when applying for this position.
