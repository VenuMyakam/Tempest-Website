We are searching for a highly creative digital business manager to lead our team with P&L responsibility. In this position, you will be responsible for all aspects of our digital marketing business.

Your central goal is to help establish the Digital Marketing Business of Tempest.

Your duties will include business development; planning, implementing, and monitoring digital marketing campaigns across all digital networks for our client’s brands.

You will lead a team of content-writers, campaign execution, social media and seo executives along with the web design and development team.

You are someone with experience in digital marketing (with experience of handling monthly Rs 25-30 lakhs ad. Budget for a brand), art direction, and social media management. In addition to being an outstanding communicator, you will also demonstrate excellent interpersonal and analytical skills.

Experience of handling international customers would be an added qualification.

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • Designing and overseeing all aspects of our digital marketing business.

  • Developing and monitoring search and social media campaign budgets.

  • Planning and managing social media platforms.

  • Preparing accurate reports on marketing campaign’s overall performance.

  • Identifying the latest trends and technologies affecting our client’s industry.

  • Evaluating important metrics that affect website traffic, service quotas, and target audience.

  • Working with our team to brainstorm new and innovative growth strategies.

Requirements:

  • Degree in marketing or relevant field.

  • A minimum of 7 to 8 years’ experience in a digital marketing.

  • In-depth knowledge of various social media platforms, best practices, and website analytics.

  • Understanding of web-technologies is required.

  • Highly creative with excellent analytical abilities.

  • Outstanding communication and interpersonal skills.

  • Up to date on the latest trends and technologies in digital marketing.
